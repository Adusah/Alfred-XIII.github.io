About me

Who is Alfred Adusah?

I am an African Entrepreneur born and raised in Ghana. Growing up and being exposed to the history of the African continent I came to appreciate how far we have come as a continent in terms of development and I was deeply motivated by the great leaders that sacrificed so much to get us here. Despite this growth I realized there was much more to be done to harness the full potential of Africa and I felt a personal burden to contribute to this development.

Later in life I further discovered my passion for entrepreneurship. Creating great business that provides so many benefits for so many people excites me immensely.

From the combination of my love for Africa and entrepreneurship my life’s purpose was born. I am here to play a major role in driving the African continent to the forefronts of the global economy through the companies I create. I call myself the great servant of Africa.
